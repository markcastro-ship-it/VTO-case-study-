The Problem

Fashion brands lose
the sale at the
product page.

Shoppers want to know how it fits, how it moves, and how it looks on someone like them — before they buy. A flat product image doesn't answer that. A return rate that's climbing does.

The average fashion brand is solving this with size charts, generic model shots, and hope. It's not working.

What Virtual Try-On Does

Tolstoy's virtual try-on lets shoppers upload a photo and see the product on themselves — directly on the PDP. No app download. No redirect. Right where the buying decision happens.

It installs as a drag-and-drop Shopify block on the product template. One setup covers every product page in the store. It can be live in minutes.

Shoppers don't need to imagine the fit.  They see it.
